Gautam Gambhir (born 14 October 1981) is an Indian former international cricketer, former politician, a philanthropist, and the current head coach[3] of the Indian cricket team in all formats. He played for India in all formats of the game between 2003 and 2016, and was considered as one of the greatest openers in his generation. He was a member of the 17th Lok Sabha from 2019 to 2024. He received the Padma Shri from the Government of India in 2019, the fourth highest civilian award in India.
